Dr. Israr Ahmad

1932 - 2010

1stAmeer of Tanzeem-e-Islami

Dr. Israr Ahmad was a Pakistani Islamic theologian, philosopher, and Islamist political figure. He was the founder of the Tanzeem-e-Islami, an Islamic organization in Pakistan that focuses on the Quranic teachings and revival of Islamic civilization. He was also a prolific author and speaker, known for his lectures and books on Islamic theology and political philosophy. Dr. Israr Ahmad passed away on April 14, 2010.

  • Dr. Israr Ahmad passed away on April 14, 2010, leaving behind a legacy as a prominent Islamic thinker and leader in Pakistan. His ideas continue to influence many people who seek to understand and practice Islam in the modern world.
